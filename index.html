About Me

I am a B.Tech pre-final year undergraduate student at the National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli, India.

​Awed by the science behind machine vision and its profound applications, I had motivated myself to learn, understand and practice Computer Vision and Deep Learning.​

I am an avid literature reader and have worked on a variety of projects, which include Image Enhancement, Segmentation, Classification problems. I will showcase some of my projects in my portfolio, and link them to their GitHub repositories.
